The Challenge

Traditional shepherding is becoming unsustainable.

With rural populations declining and manual labor costs reaching up to 3-4 times the minimum wage, managing medium to large-scale livestock operations is harder than ever. Simple GPS collars and virtual fences aren't enough to actively manage and protect a moving herd.

Labor Shortages

Finding reliable, affordable human shepherds for 6-8 month grazing periods is a growing bottleneck.

Herd Stress & Loss

Lack of continuous, analytical monitoring leads to unnoticed animal stress, predator threats, and financial loss.

Our Solution

The RoboÇoban Ecosystem

A coordinated framework where aerial and ground vehicles work together as a single unit to provide complete safety and supervision of the herd while grazing.

  • Cuts labor expenses by ~50%
  • Prevents animal loss via continuous monitoring
  • Monthly efficiency and herd health ROI reports
  • Scalable for farms of any size

Powered by Advanced AI

A Unified Autonomous Platform

We don't just track GPS. We analyze social hierarchy, detect anomalies, and actively guide the herd using military-grade robotics adapted for agriculture.

Aerial Surveillance (UAV)

Our drone scans from above using infrared sensors. It acts as the "eye in the sky," detecting environmental hazards and tracking the herd's overall movement.

Ground Rover (UGV)

Trained with Reinforcement Learning (RL) to navigate rough terrain. It utilizes RT-DETR for precision tracking and active herd deterrence on the ground.

Behavioral AI

Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) analyze herd psychology. We identify leader animals and instantly detect if the flock is under stress or facing a threat.

Real-Time Mobile App

A cross-platform Flutter app synced via Firebase backend. Monitor your herd's status, view stress reports, and control the autonomous fleet from your pocket.
